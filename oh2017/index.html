Posted on 12th December 201620th November 2017 by Eunoia JC

We are Ready to Welcome Our First Class

Swing by our Open House, OH EUNOIA!, to find out more about Eunoia Junior College. Join us in taster lessons, walk through our CCA carnival, tour our innovative learning spaces, and meet your future collaborators. In just one day, terms like 5i Curriculum, Passion Pursuit, Student Voice, and more will excite and energise you. We are confident you will be ready to create the Eunoia legacy. Take your first step on 13 January. Join our First Class!

Getting Here

Meet Us at Buona Vista

We are located at 53 Mount Sinai Road, Singapore 276880. The nearest MRT station is Buona Vista station, located on the East-West line (EW21) and the Circle line (CC22). A short walk will take you from the station to the college campus.

 

Public Transport

You can also board bus service 92 from Buona Vista station and alight at the third bus stop, 112131 (Aft Grove Rd). Other bus services that stop in the vicinity of the college include: 100 and 111 from Ghim Moh Bus Terminal, 105, 106, 147 and 185 along Commonwealth Ave West, and 7, 61, 75, 156 and 165 along Holland Road.

 

Driving to Mount Sinai

By car, you can reach 53 Mount Sinai Road via Holland Road or Commonwealth Ave West. From Holland Road, turn into Holland Grove Road (Henry Park Primary School) and turn left onto Mount Sinai Road. From Commonwealth Ave West, drive along Ghim Moh Rd, past Ulu Panda CC and turn right before the zebra crossing. Alternative parking is available at the Ghim Moh Green HDB estate (near Block 1) and near Ghim Moh Market & Food Centre (Blocks 7-8).
